INTELLIGENT APPROACH FOR CLASSIFICATION OF OSTEOPOROSIS
About

Intelligence appraoch for classification of Osteoporosis of the knee is a machine learning project aimed at classifying knee X-ray images into different categories of osteoporosis. The project utilizes a deep learning model to accurately predict the severity of osteoporosis in the knee, providing valuable insights for early detection and treatment.
